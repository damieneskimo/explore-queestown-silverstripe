TANDEM SKYDIVE QUEENSTOWN

  • Info
  • Price
  • Tips

Overcome the natural urge of self-preservation and jump out of a plane into thin air. It takes a certain kind of person to embrace the fear - it takes courage! Skydiving is an incredible feeling of exhilaration, the adrenalin is addictive and the sense of personal achievement astounding.

Get ready for up to 60 seconds of free-fall as your tandem jump master throws you out of a plane, plummeting towards the ground before parachuting gently into land. Free-falling at 200km/h never felt so good - terminal velocity!

You will be provided with a jumpsuit, hat, goggles and gloves, so all you need to wear are warm layers and flat closed-toed shoes. If you wear glasses, Nzone have goggles that fit over the top.

Skydiving is very weather dependant & can get called off easily. If you want to Skydive & are in town for a few days plan to do it on day one, this way if it gets postponed by a day or two you wont miss out.

Go big or go home. You wont regret going higher!

If jumping in winter, an extra layer of thermal wear would not go astray - it's pretty damn cold at altitude but totally worth it!
